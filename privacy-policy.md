# Privacy Policy

**Louisiana French** — Alexa Skill

*Last updated: March 10, 2026*

## Overview

Louisiana French is an Alexa skill that provides dictionary lookups and translations for Louisiana French (Cajun and Creole French). Your privacy is important to us, and this skill is designed to be as simple and transparent as possible.

## Data Collection

This skill does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- We do not collect your name, email, location, or any identifying information.
- We do not store your voice requests or search history.
- We do not use account linking or require any login.
- We do not make calls to external servers — all dictionary data is bundled within the skill itself.

## Amazon and Alexa

When you use this skill, Amazon processes your voice input through the Alexa service. Amazon's collection and use of that data is governed by the [Amazon Privacy Notice](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496) and the [Alexa Terms of Use](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=201809740).

## Children's Privacy

This skill does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date.
